Bracket Contests

How they work

To play in a Bracket Contest you must predict the playoffs by picking the winner of each playoff series in exact games. The more accurate your predictions, the more points you earn. To win a bracket Contest you must earn more points than anyone in your Contest. Get more info about Bracket Contests in the video below!

The playoff formats are different for every sports league. So please visit any of the pages below to learn the specific scoring system used in Bracket Contests for the NBA, NHL, and MLB playoffs.
