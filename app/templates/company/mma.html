MMA Contests

How it works

Before each MMA event you predict the outcomes of all the fights. The more accurate your predictions the more points your earn. After the MMA event ends count up your points. If you earned more points than your opponent then all the CASH and bragging rights are yours!

Read below for more detail on the MMA contest scoring system.

 

SCORING SYSTEM

PRELIMS: Pick winning fighter in Prelim Bout and earn 6 points
MAIN CARD: Pick winning fighter in Main Card Bout and earn 12 points
MAIN EVENT: Pick winning fighter in Main Event (or Co Main Event) and earn 24 points

 

Double Your Points for Correct Fight Pick if...

You predict the winning fighter and the correct method of victory (KO/TKO, Decision, or Submission)

 

Triple Your Points for Correct Fight Pick if...

You predict the winning fighter and the correct method of victory in the correct round.

 

** IMPORTANT Zero points awarded for any fights that end in draws or disqualification!
