MLB Draft Contests

How it works

Draft Contests are season long Pick’em Contests. So before the season starts there is a scheduled draft. Each person in the draft ends up with same number of MLB teams. At the end of the MLB season there are two winners.

Winner #1 -- The person that accumulates most wins from their drafted MLB teams.

Winner #2 -- The person that drafts the World Series Winner.

It's literally that simple.

MLB Bracket Contests

How it works

You must predict the entire playoffs by picking the winner of each MLB playoff series in exact games. The more accurate your predictions the more points you earn. Read below for more detail on the point system.

Each round of Playoffs has a total value of 32 points:

Wildcard Game: Each Wild Card Winner is worth 4 Points.
Divisional Playoffs: Each Divisional Series Winner is worth 8 points.
League Championship Series: ALCS Winner and NLCS Winner each is worth 16 points.
World Series: World Series Winner is worth 32 Points.


Double Your Points for Series win if…
Receive double points for a Playoff series if you predict correct series winner in exact number of games.

Triple Your Points for Series win if...
Receive triple points for Playoff series if you predict correct series winner, against correct opponent in exact number of games.**

**Triple bonus only available in National League Championship Series, American League Championship Series and the World Series.
