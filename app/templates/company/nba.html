NBA Draft Contests

How it works

Draft Contests are season long Pick’em Contests. So before the season starts there is a scheduled draft. Each person in the draft ends up with same number of NBA teams. At the end of the NBA season there are two winners.

Winner #1 -- The person that accumulates most wins from their drafted NBA teams.

Winner #2 -- The person that drafts NBA Finals Winner.

It's literally that simple.

NBA Bracket Contests

How it works

You must predict the entire playoffs by picking the winner of each NBA playoff series in exact games. The more accurate your predictions the more points you earn. Read below for more detail on the point system.

Each round of Playoffs has a total value of 32 points:

First Round: Each First Round Series Winner is worth 4 Points
Conference Semifinals: Each Conference Semifinals Winner is worth 8 points.
Conference Finals: The Western Conference Winner and Eastern Conference Winner each is worth 16 points.
NBA Finals: NBA Finals Winner is worth 32 Points.

Double Your Points for Series win if…
Receive double points for a Playoff series if you predict correct series winner in exact number of games.

Triple Your Points for Series win if...
Receive triple points for Playoff series if you predict correct series winner, against correct opponent in exact number of games.**

**Triple bonus not available in First round since seeds/matchups already determined.
