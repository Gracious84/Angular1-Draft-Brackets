It's simple, we offer two types of contests DRAFTS and BRACKETS (bold, same border/outline as homepage for both DRAFTS and BRACKETs).

DRAFTS

There is a scheduled draft. Each person in the draft ends up with same number of teams. At the end of the contest there are two winners.

Winner #1 -- The person that accumulates most wins from their drafted teams.

Winner #2 -- The person that drafts League Champion (Super Bowl Winner, World Series Winner, etc).

It's literally that simple.
